Introduction

Access to legal information and awareness is a challenge for a large population in India, especially for those who are not literate or are from marginalized communities. Our aim was to develop a digital assistant that can provide legal information and guidance to people in a user-friendly manner. The digital assistant would be able to converse in multiple languages and provide information in a concise and easy-to-understand manner. The platform should be accessible through various devices, including smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers.
